— CHAPTER III. SOVEREIGNTY

On ownership.

A SHORT INSTRUMENT IN PLACE OF A PRIVACY POLICY

  1. ARTICLE I. The subject retains full ownership of all data, derivative weights, and behavioral artifacts produced within the engagement, in perpetuity.
  2. ARTICLE II. No model is shared across subjects. No weights are pooled. No corpus leaves the engagement for any purpose, including our own.
  3. ARTICLE III. Hardware resides on infrastructure of the subject's election — private cloud, sovereign region, or personal hardware — never on shared tenancy by default.
  4. ARTICLE IV. On termination, the entire system — weights, embeddings, logs, keys — is delivered to the subject in portable form and destroyed from our custody in the same gesture.
  5. ARTICLE V. The practice holds the name and the substance of every engagement in confidence, without expiration.
